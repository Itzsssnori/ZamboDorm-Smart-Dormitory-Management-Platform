Payments & Billing
When is rent due each month?
Rent is due on the 7th of every month. If the 7th falls on a weekend or holiday, payment is still expected by that date or the business day prior. You will receive an in-app reminder 5 days and 1 day before the due date.
Was this helpful?
What payment methods are accepted?
We currently accept the following payment methods:
  • GCash (recommended)
  • Maya (PayMaya)
  • Bank Transfer – BDO & BPI
  • Cash payment at the reception office
All online payments are reflected in your dashboard within 1–2 hours.
Was this helpful?
What happens if I miss the payment deadline?
A late fee of ₱200 is charged for every 5 days past the due date. Accounts more than 30 days overdue may be subject to service suspension. Please contact the office immediately if you're unable to pay on time — we can often arrange a grace period.
Was this helpful?
How do I request a payment receipt?
Go to Payments → Payment History, find the transaction, and click Download Receipt. Receipts are in PDF format. For cash payments, an official receipt is issued at the time of payment by the reception officer.
Was this helpful?
Can I pay in advance for multiple months?
Yes! You may pay up to 3 months in advance. Advance payments are recorded in your account and automatically applied each month. To pay in advance, go to Payments → Pay Now and select the months you wish to cover.
Was this helpful?
Why is my payment not reflecting in the dashboard?
Online payments typically reflect within 1–2 hours. If your payment has not appeared after 24 hours, please send your proof of payment to support@zambodorm.com or present it at the reception. Cash payments are posted manually and may take until the next business day.

Visitor Policies
What are the visiting hours?
Visiting hours are 8:00 AM to 9:00 PM, seven days a week. All visitors must arrive and depart within this window. No visitors are permitted to stay overnight under any circumstances.
Was this helpful?
How do I register a visitor?
Go to Visitors in the sidebar → click Register New Visitor → fill out the 3-step form with your visitor's name, contact, ID, purpose, and expected check-in time. Submit for admin approval. Your visitor may only enter once approved.
Was this helpful?
How many visitors can I have at once?
Each tenant may have a maximum of 2 approved visitors at any given time. Additional visitor registrations will be placed on hold until an existing visitor checks out.
Was this helpful?
What is the gender policy for visitors?
Only same-gender visitors are permitted in the dormitory rooms and floors. This policy is strictly enforced by reception staff. Exceptions may be granted for immediate family members (e.g., parents or siblings) with prior admin approval.
Was this helpful?
What ID does my visitor need to present?
Visitors must present any valid government-issued ID: National ID, Passport, Driver's License, PhilSys card, UMID, or Voter's ID. The ID number must match what was registered in the system. School IDs are not accepted.

Services & Requests
What services can I request through the app?
You can request the following services through the app:
  • Water Delivery – 5-gallon drinking water containers
  • Laundry Service – Wash & fold or dry cleaning
Go to Request Service in the sidebar to place a request.
Was this helpful?
How long does a service request take?
Water delivery is usually fulfilled within 2–4 hours of approval. Laundry service turnaround is 24–48 hours depending on volume. You'll receive a notification when your request status changes.
Was this helpful?
How do I report a maintenance issue?
Go to Complaints in the sidebar → select the issue category (electrical, plumbing, air conditioning, etc.) → describe the problem and optionally attach a photo. Urgent issues like flooding or power outages should also be reported directly to reception.
Was this helpful?
Can I cancel a service request?
Yes, you can cancel a service request if its status is still Pending. Go to Request Service → My Requests, find the request, and click Cancel. Once a request is In Progress or Completed, cancellation is no longer available.
Was this helpful?
Account & Settings
How do I change my password?
Go to Settings → Security tab → Change Password. You'll need to enter your current password, then set a new one. Make sure your new password is at least 8 characters and includes a mix of letters, numbers, and symbols.
Was this helpful?
How do I update my contact information?
Go to Settings → Profile tab. You can update your name, email, phone number, and emergency contact. Click Save Changes when done. Email changes may require re-verification.
Was this helpful?
I forgot my password. How do I recover my account?
On the login page, click Forgot Password and enter your registered email address. A password reset link will be sent to your inbox. The link expires in 30 minutes. If you don't receive the email, check your spam folder or contact support.
Was this helpful?
How do I turn off email notifications?
Go to Settings → Notifications tab. You can toggle individual notification types on or off. Note that Payment Reminders are strongly recommended to keep — turning them off may result in missed due dates.
Was this helpful?
Rules & Policies
What is the curfew policy?
Dorm curfew is at 10:00 PM on weekdays and 11:00 PM on weekends. Tenants arriving after curfew must sign the late entry logbook at reception. Repeated late entries may result in a formal warning.
Was this helpful?
Are appliances allowed in rooms?
Tenants may bring and register approved appliances. Allowed: electric fans, desk lamps, laptop/phone chargers, and small personal appliances. Not allowed: electric stoves, rice cookers, flat irons used inside rooms, and high-wattage devices. All appliances must be registered in My Room → Items & Appliances.
Was this helpful?
What is the noise policy?
Quiet hours are enforced from 10:00 PM to 6:00 AM. Playing loud music, shouting, or any disruptive noise during these hours is prohibited. Violations are logged and may result in a formal complaint against the tenant.
Was this helpful?
What happens if I violate dorm rules?
The violation escalation process is:
  • 1st offense: Verbal warning
  • 2nd offense: Written warning + note in tenant file
  • 3rd offense: Formal notice with possible lease impact
  • Serious violations: Immediate escalation and possible eviction
Was this helpful?
How do I check out / end my lease?
Give at least 30 days written notice before your intended move-out date. This can be done by submitting a notice through the Complaints form or speaking to the reception office. Settlement of all outstanding balances and a room inspection are required before checkout.
