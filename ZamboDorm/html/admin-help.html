Tenant Management
How do I onboard a new tenant?
Go to Tenants → Add New Tenant. Fill in personal details (full name, contact, emergency contact, valid ID), assign a room from the available units list, set the lease start date and monthly rate, then click Create Account. The system will auto-generate login credentials and send them to the tenant's email.
Make sure the room is set to Available before assigning — occupied rooms cannot be double-assigned.

How do I transfer a tenant to a different room?
Go to Tenants → [Tenant Profile] → Room Assignment → Transfer Room. Select the destination room (must be available) and set the transfer date. The old room's status will automatically update to Vacant. Billing adjustments, if any, must be made manually in the Payments section.
Was this helpful?
How do I process a tenant checkout / end of lease?
Navigate to Tenants → [Tenant Profile] → End Lease. The system will show outstanding balances before proceeding. Steps:
  • Confirm all payments are settled
  • Log the room inspection result
  • Deduct or refund security deposit as needed
  • Click Confirm Checkout — this archives the tenant and marks the room Vacant
Checkout cannot be undone. Archived tenants are read-only.

How do I edit a tenant's personal information?
Go to Tenants → [Tenant Profile] → Edit Profile. You can update name, contact number, emergency contact, and valid ID on file. Email changes require the tenant to re-verify their address. Changes are logged in the tenant's audit trail.
Was this helpful?
How do I suspend or restrict a tenant's account?
Go to Tenants → [Tenant Profile] → Account Status → Suspend Account. A suspended tenant cannot log in or submit requests. You must provide a suspension reason, which is recorded in the tenant's file. Suspension does not cancel rent obligations — billing continues unless the lease is formally ended.
Suspension is visible to the tenant when they attempt to log in. Use with caution and ensure proper documentation.

How do I approve or reject a visitor registration?
Go to Visitor Logs → Pending Approvals. Each card shows the visitor's name, ID type, tenant being visited, purpose, and expected time. Click Approve to allow entry or Reject with an optional reason. Rejected registrations notify the tenant automatically. You can also filter by date, tenant, or status.

Payments & Billing
How do I manually record a cash payment?
Go to Payments → Record Payment. Select the tenant, choose Cash as the payment method, enter the amount and reference (receipt number), then click Post Payment. The payment will immediately reflect in the tenant's account and reduce any outstanding balance. Issue a physical official receipt to the tenant.
Was this helpful?
How do I verify and approve an online payment?
Go to Payments → Pending Verification. Online payments (GCash, Maya, bank transfer) appear here with the tenant's uploaded proof of payment. Cross-check the reference number and amount, then click Verify & Approve. The tenant is notified once approved. If incorrect, click Reject and provide a reason.
Always verify against your e-wallet / bank dashboard before approving. Do not rely solely on the tenant's screenshot.
Was this helpful?
How do I apply or waive a late fee?
Late fees (₱200 per 5 days overdue) are applied automatically by the system. To waive a late fee, go to Payments → [Tenant] → Balance Details → Adjustments → Waive Fee. You must enter a reason (e.g. approved grace period). Waivers are logged and cannot be reversed once saved.
Was this helpful?
How do I generate a billing statement or payment report?
Go to Payments → Reports. Select the date range and report type:
  • Monthly Collection Summary – total collected per payment method
  • Outstanding Balances – all tenants with unpaid dues
  • Individual Statement – per-tenant billing history (exportable to PDF)
Click Generate Report and download as PDF or CSV.
Was this helpful?
How do I adjust the monthly rate for a specific tenant?
Go to Tenants → [Tenant Profile] → Lease Details → Edit Rate. Enter the new monthly rate and the effective date. If mid-month, a pro-rated adjustment is generated automatically for that billing period. Changes are reflected in the tenant's next payment notification.
Rate changes should be communicated to the tenant in writing before taking effect.

Operations
How do I manage and respond to service requests?
Go to Service Requests. All pending requests (water delivery, laundry) appear in a queue sorted by date. Click a request to view details, then update the status:
  • Pending → In Progress – assign staff and begin fulfillment
  • In Progress → Completed – mark as done; tenant is notified
  • Reject – only use if the request cannot be fulfilled; add a reason
You can filter by service type, date, or status.
Was this helpful?
How do I handle and resolve tenant complaints?
Go to Complaints. Each complaint shows the category (maintenance, noise, etc.), description, optional photo, and submission date. Update the status to Under Review while investigating, then Resolved once addressed. You can add an internal admin note visible only to staff, and a resolution note visible to the tenant.
Was this helpful?
How do I manage room status (vacant, occupied, under maintenance)?
Go to Rooms. The floor map or list view shows all rooms with color-coded status badges. To change a room's status manually, click the room → Edit Status → select from:
  • Occupied – assigned to an active tenant
  • Vacant – available for assignment
  • Under Maintenance – temporarily unavailable; include expected end date
  • Reserved – held for an incoming tenant
Was this helpful?
How do I view and export the visitor logbook?
Go to Visitor Logs → All Logs. The table shows every visitor entry with check-in time, check-out time, tenant visited, and ID type. You can filter by date range and tenant. Click Export CSV to download the log for record-keeping or security audits.
Was this helpful?
How do I send an announcement or notice to all tenants?
Go to Dashboard → Announcements → New Announcement. Write the subject and body. You can target:
  • All Tenants – broadcast to everyone
  • Specific Floor / Room – targeted message
  • Tenants with Outstanding Balance – billing reminders
Choose delivery method (in-app notification, email, or both) and click Send.

Violations & Escalations
How do I log a rule violation against a tenant?
Go to Tenants → [Tenant Profile] → Violation Log → Add Violation. Select the violation type (noise, curfew, unauthorized appliance, visitor policy, etc.), set the date and severity, add a description, and optionally attach evidence. The system automatically tracks offense count and flags if the 3rd offense threshold is reached.
Violations are visible to the tenant in their account history under "Notices."
Was this helpful?
How do I issue a formal written warning?
After logging the violation, go to Violation Log → [Violation] → Issue Written Warning. A pre-formatted warning letter template will load with the tenant's details and the violation description. Edit as needed, then click Send & Archive. The letter is emailed to the tenant and stored in their file as a PDF.
Was this helpful?
What is the escalation process for serious violations?
For serious violations (unauthorized persons, threats, property damage), skip standard steps and go directly to Tenants → [Tenant Profile] → Escalate Case. This flags the account for urgent review and notifies the dorm management head. Escalated cases are reviewed within 24 hours and may result in immediate lease termination per dorm policy.
Lease termination requires documented evidence and must follow due process as stated in the tenant's contract.

How do I view all active violations across tenants?
Go to Dashboard → Violation Overview. This shows a summary table of all tenants with open violations, offense count, and last logged date. You can filter by violation type or severity. Tenants at 2nd or 3rd offense are highlighted in amber or red respectively for quick identification.

System & Settings
How do I add or remove an admin staff account?
Go to Settings → Staff Accounts → Add Staff. Enter the staff member's name, email, and assign a role:
  • Super Admin – full access including settings and staff management
  • Staff – access to operations, complaints, and visitor logs; cannot change settings or billing rates
To remove an account, click the staff member → Deactivate Account. Deactivated accounts cannot log in but their action history is preserved.
Was this helpful?
How do I update dorm-wide settings (billing cycle, late fee, curfew)?
Go to Settings → Dorm Configuration. From here you can update:
  • Billing due day – default is the 7th of every month
  • Late fee amount and interval – default ₱200 per 5 days
  • Curfew times – weekday and weekend independently
  • Visiting hours – default 8AM–9PM
  • Max concurrent visitors per tenant
All changes take effect at the next billing cycle or the following day for curfew/visitor settings.
Was this helpful?
How do I view the system audit log?
Go to Settings → Audit Log. This lists all admin actions with timestamp, staff name, and action description (e.g. payment.verified, tenant.suspended, room.status_changed). You can filter by date range or staff member. The log is read-only and cannot be deleted.
Audit logs are retained for 12 months and can be exported as CSV for compliance purposes.
Was this helpful?
How do I back up or export system data?
Go to Settings → Data & Backup. You can export the following as CSV or PDF:
  • Full tenant list with room assignments
  • Payment history (all or date range)
  • Visitor log archive
  • Violation records
Automated nightly backups run at 2:00 AM and are stored server-side for 30 days. Contact your developer to restore from a backup.
Exported data contains personal information. Handle and store securely per your data privacy obligations.
