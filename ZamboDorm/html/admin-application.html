What You'll Need

Prepare the following documents and information before filling out the application. All items marked Required must be submitted.

🪪

Valid Government-Issued ID

Passport, PhilSys (National ID), Driver's License, UMID, Voter's ID, or PRC ID. Must be valid and not expired.

Required
📋

Issued Document of Validity

Proof of dormitory authority — Business Permit, Barangay Clearance, or Department of Tourism accreditation.

Required
🏠

Photos of the Dormitory

At least 3 clear exterior and interior photos of the dormitory premises, including common areas and sample rooms.

Required
🤳

Photo of the Administrator

A clear recent photo of yourself (face clearly visible), similar to a passport-style photo. No filters or sunglasses.

Required
📍

Dorm Location & Contact Info

Full address, Google Maps pin or coordinates, contact number, and social media page (if available).

Required
📄

Dorm House Rules (optional)

Upload your existing house rules or policies. You can also set these up later through your admin dashboard.
