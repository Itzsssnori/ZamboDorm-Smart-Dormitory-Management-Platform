Frequently Asked Questions
How-to guides for all 9 admin sections
The Overview dashboard gives you a real-time snapshot of your dormitory: Total Tenants, Available Rooms, Pending Payments, and Active Complaints. It also displays a Revenue Analysis bar chart for the last 5 months and an Occupancy Rate donut chart so you can assess dorm health at a glance.
Go to Tenants in the sidebar. Click Add Tenant to onboard a new resident — fill in their personal details, assign a room, and set the lease start date and monthly rate. To edit, click on any tenant's name and update their record. To remove, open the tenant record and click Move Out to archive the record and free the room.
Go to Rooms in the sidebar to see the full occupancy tracker. Each room card shows its number, floor, occupancy status (Occupied, Vacant, Under Maintenance), current tenant, and monthly rate. Click any room to edit its details, mark it as available or under maintenance, or reassign it to a different tenant. Vacant rooms are highlighted so you can fill them quickly.
Go to Payments in the sidebar. You'll see all tenants with their current balance, due dates, and payment status. Click Mark as Paid on a pending payment, enter the reference number and date, and confirm. Overdue balances are flagged in red. You can also filter by month or export a billing report as CSV for your records.
Go to Service Requests in the sidebar. All maintenance requests and anonymous complaints appear here. Open any request to review the issue category and description. Update the status to In Progress or Resolved, add a response note, and optionally assign it to a staff member. The tenant is notified on every status change.
Go to Announcements and click New Announcement. Set the title, message body, and priority level (High, Medium, or Low). Once posted, all tenants see it immediately on their dashboard and in their Help & Announcements pages. To update dorm rules or curfew hours, simply post a new announcement labeled clearly — tenants will see the update instantly.
Go to Visitor Logs in the sidebar. You'll see all registered visitor entries submitted by tenants, including visitor name, relationship, host tenant, expected date and time, and check-in/check-out timestamps. Filter by date range or search by tenant name. Flag suspicious entries and export the full log as CSV for security records.
Go to Room Gallery in the sidebar. You can view all uploaded room photos organized by room number. Click any room to upload new photos, replace existing ones, or delete outdated images. Gallery photos are visible to prospective tenants on the landing page and to current tenants in their My Room section.
Go to Admin Settings at the bottom of the sidebar. From here you can update dorm information (name, address, contact), set default monthly rates, configure notification preferences, manage admin accounts and permissions, and adjust system-wide settings like currency and timezone. Changes take effect immediately across the platform.
