Frequently Asked Questions
Common questions from tenants
Go to the Payments page from the sidebar. You'll see your current balance and a list of billing history. Click Pay Bill on any unpaid month, choose your preferred payment method (GCash, Maya, or Bank Transfer), enter your reference number, and submit. You'll receive a confirmation once processed.
Navigate to Request Service in the sidebar. Fill in the issue category (e.g. Electrical, Plumbing) and describe the problem in the text box. Once submitted, the admin will review and schedule a repair. You'll be notified when the status changes.
Go to Visitors in the sidebar and fill in your visitor's name, relationship, and expected visit date and time. Once submitted, the guard on duty will be notified. Visitors must present a valid ID upon entry.
Go to Complaints in the sidebar. Select Noise Complaint as the issue category and describe the situation in detail. All reports are submitted anonymously to admin. For urgent situations, contact the front desk directly using the number below.
Click My Room in the sidebar. You'll find your room number, floor, lease start and end dates, monthly rate, and included amenities. If any details are incorrect, please contact the admin.
Click Settings at the bottom of the sidebar. From there you can update your notification preferences, language, and display settings. To change your password, go to the Privacy & Security section and click Change Password.
Curfew hours are set by the dorm administration and may vary depending on circumstances. As a general guideline, it is advisable to follow a 10:00 PM curfew for weekdays. Visitors are welcome during the day but are not allowed to stay overnight. Please refer to the dorm’s official house rules posted at the front desk for complete details.
